OUR PROJECT

Political education is crucial for fostering informed and effective participation in political life, yet its deficiency poses a significant democratic challenge, notably evident in electoral abstentionism, which undermines the legitimacy of democratic processes and diminishes the diversity of voices in public discourse. Through interactive sessions spanning consecutive days, students engage in discussions and voting, illustrating the global impact of their choices and encouraging debate within the local community. By gradually transitioning from simple to more complex questions, we aim to underscore the importance of voting and demonstrate the growth of student participation throughout the projectÂ duration.
